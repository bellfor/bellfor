&lt;b&gt;&lt;i&gt;Collagen&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/b&gt;

and bones. It is synthesized to heal injuries.&lt;br&gt;
Collagen of type I is more easily absorbed and more digestible than collagen of
type II.&lt;br&gt;
Collagen type I is essential for the stability and regeneration of cartilage.
In addition, collagen type I activates the synthesis of hyaluronic acid and
endogenous collagen in cartilage, which essentially contributes to the
improvement of mobility.&amp;nbsp;&lt;br&gt;
&lt;br&gt;
&lt;b&gt;&lt;i&gt;Hyaluronic
&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/b&gt;acid as the main ingredient is responsible for the liquid elasticity of
the cartilage and lubrication of joints by synovial fluid. In addition, it
protects the articular cartilage against wear and tear, reduces inflammation
and facilitates the development of cartilage. Various studies have found that
the high concentration of hyaluronic acid (650 KDa), which can be found in our
Bellfor Joints &amp;amp; Bones, protects and improves the function of joints.&lt;br&gt;
&lt;br&gt;
&lt;b&gt;&lt;i&gt;Glucosamine
&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/b&gt;plays a key role in the preservation of the cartilage. It activates the
synthesis of collagen, endogenous chondroitin and a precursor of
glucosamine.&amp;nbsp;Glucosamine can thus stop the progression of symptoms, which
are caused by arthritis and osteoarthritis.&lt;br&gt;
&amp;nbsp;&lt;br&gt;
&lt;b&gt;&lt;i&gt;Chondroitin
sulphate acts as an effective protective coating for the cartilage, the
membrane which encapsulates the synovial fluid, and the bones.&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/b&gt; These
are the three main structures that are damaged in arthritis. In addition.
chondroitin sulphate can stop and curb some of the factors that cause
inflammatory processes.&lt;br&gt;
